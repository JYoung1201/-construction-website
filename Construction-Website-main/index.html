Building with Faith and Expertise

Tramm Construction is a family-owned and operated business built on the foundation of Christian values. Founded in 1991 by Marlyn Tramm, a dedicated educator who initially ran the company alongside his teaching career. He then passed the business onto his son, Kevin, who ensures Tramm Construction delivers the highest standards for every project. Today, Kevin and his business partner Mark Waters lead Tramm Construction, carrying on Kevin's father's legacy of excellence.
